Welcome to the Archive of the Lost Voices

Who are we?

We are a collective of individuals from various backgrounds celebrating black identity shown in Film, Music, History and Fashion. Our main aim is to celebrate all black identities and other voices that have been blurred out in main stream media. We occasionally host 'get2gether' with a different theme, last month was y2k and the month before that was 'come as a celebrity'.
